Janken (じゃんけん) is the Japanese equivalent of Rock Paper Scissors. The "children's game" is the means of settling all manner of disputes, disagreements, and debates.

  • Who gets to eat the last pocky? Janken
  • Who goes first in a game of sugoroku? Janken
  • What auction house should auction off my multi-million dollar art collection? Janken...no really
